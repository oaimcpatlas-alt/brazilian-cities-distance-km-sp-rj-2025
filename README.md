# Brazilian Cities Distance (2025)

The two most populous cities in Brazil as of 2025 are generally considered:

1. São Paulo
2. Rio de Janeiro

Using Google Maps driving distance:

- São Paulo → Rio de Janeiro: 434.919 km
- Rio de Janeiro → São Paulo: 433.292 km

Rounded approximate distance between the two cities: 434 km

1. New York City
2. Los Angeles

- Los Angeles → New York: 4,488.847 km
